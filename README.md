# MelloMinds.co.in

**Next-Gen Therapy Practice Management Platform**

MelloMinds is a specialized SaaS platform designed to streamline the operational, clinical, and financial workflows for mental health professionals, clinics, and therapists. It serves as a unified command center to manage clients, appointments, clinical notes, and billing with a focus on data privacy and ease of use.

---

## 🚀 Vision

To empower mental health professionals by reducing administrative burnout, ensuring data security, and providing seamless client experiences through intelligent practice management.

**Current Phase:** Phase 1 (MVP) - Single Provider Platform.

---

## ✨ Key Features (Phase 1)

### 🔐 Authentication & Security
- **Secure Access:** Email/Password & Google OAuth login.
- **Onboarding:** Professional profile setup (License info, Specialization).
- **Compliance:** Designed with HIPAA/GDPR data structure standards.

### 📊 Practice Dashboard
- **KPI Overview:** Real-time view of upcoming sessions, active clients, and monthly revenue.
- **Action Stream:** Notifications for unpaid invoices and pending clinical notes.

### 📇 Client CRM
- **Digital Charts:** Centralized client profiles with contact details and history.
- **Session Logs:** Chronological view of all past and upcoming interactions.
- **Document Management:** Secure storage for client intake forms and files.

### 🗓️ Smart Scheduling
- **Calendar Management:** Drag-and-drop appointment handling.
- **2-Way Sync:** Integration with Google Calendar to prevent double-booking.
- **Shareable Booking Links:** Custom URLs for clients to self-book based on availability rules.

### 📝 Clinical Documentation
- **Session Notes:** Secure, rich-text editor for SOAP/DAP notes.
- **Locking Mechanism:** Audit-proof "Lock" feature for finalized notes.

### 💸 Billing & Invoicing
- **Invoicing:** Auto-generation of invoices post-session.
- **Payment Tracking:** Log payments via Stripe integration or manual entry (Cash/Bank Transfer).
- **Financial Reports:** Track paid vs. overdue invoices.
